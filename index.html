Justin L. Ripley

Current position

I am currently a postdoctoral research associate at the Department of Physics at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign.

From 2020-2022 I was a postdoctoral research associate at the Department of Applied Math and Theoretical Physics at the University of Cambridge.

My research focuses on developing (numerical) methods that can be used to test General Relativity in black hole and cosmological spacetimes. I frequently use the tools of numerical relativity and gravitational perturbation theory.

Education

I obtained my PhD from the Department of Physics, Princeton University, under the supervision of Frans Pretorius. A pdf of my thesis, General Relativity and its classical modification in gravitational collapse, can be found here.
